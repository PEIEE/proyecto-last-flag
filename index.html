The Rules

Hide. Hunt. Capture.

You've got 60 seconds to find a sneaky hiding spot for your flag. After that, it's SHOWTIME! Work with your allies to take control of the map and stop the other team from finding your flag, all while hunting down theirs. The first team to bring home the opposing flag and successfully defend it will claim the glory.

🚩

Your Playstyle

Play Your Way

Cashbot crusher, radar tower tycoon, flag-running phenom, all-out brawler — how will you rise to stardom? Sure, sharp aim earns applause, but there are endless ways to push your team closer to victory. Lean into your strengths, lock in a play style that's uniquely yours, and start racking up MVP moments.

The Roster

Killer Contestants

Master a legendary cast of contestants, each armed with primetime firepower and signature abilities you can wield to outwit, outplay, and outgun the competition.

Peter Makinen

Frontline Bruiser

Lumberjack

Gentle giant from the Finnish wilderness who fights with the mythical Mäkinen axe and smiles through it all. Resilient, unshakably calm — he shrugs off punishment and clears the path with raw strength.

  • Primary Dive Roll
  • Secondary Grand Slam
  • Ultimate Forest Wrath

Shin Soo-Jin

Stealth Infiltrator

Knives

The lethal, masked assassin known as Knives strikes from the shadows and vanishes before the smoke clears. Fast, relentless, and unforgiving — if she has her sights on you, it’s already over.

  • Primary Blade Rush
  • Secondary Going Dark
  • Ultimate Shadowlight Portals

Camila Barbosa

Zone Controller

Arsenal

Genius inventor with a gleeful disregard for safety. Top of her class and only slightly unhinged — she floods the map with aggressive contraptions and corrosive tech.

  • Primary Tesla Turret
  • Secondary Healing Station
  • Ultimate Tornado Grenade

Julius Reeves

Mobile All-Rounder

Bounty Hunter

Bounty hunter, showman, part-time myth — Julius rides into every match with flair and firepower on his custom Z1000 motorcycle. Always playing for the highlight reel.

  • Primary Seeker Grenades
  • Secondary Jail Time
  • Ultimate Brass Belcher Minigun

Hazuki Masako

Precision Fighter

Banshee

Heavy-metal frontwoman with deadly aim and a legendary temper. A high-risk, high-reward fighter who demands perfect shots — she's not here to win, she's here to dominate.

  • Primary Spectral Scream
  • Secondary Banshee Dash
  • Ultimate Healing Arrow

Jack Burtt

Support Tactician

Roadie

Touring roadie turned battlefield engineer — builds, boosts, and blows things up with a backstage pass. His magnet-powered grenade launcher literally makes noise. Lots of it.

  • Ultimate Mosh Pit

Parker Spencer

Expert Tracker

Scout

Expert tracker and crack shot trained by his forest ranger grandfather. Supported by his cybernetic falcon Luther, he keeps eyes on the whole field and covers ground with breathtaking speed.

  • Secondary Falcon Sprint
  • Ultimate Metal Storm

Alejandro

On Last Flag for one reason only — to find his missing sister Maria Elana. Armed with an assault rifle, electric bolas, and proximity mines. No one is stopping him.
