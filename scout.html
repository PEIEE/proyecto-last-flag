About Parker Spencer

A master of close-quarters combat and stealth, Parker Spencer was a prodigious talent in the underground martial arts scene in Seoul. Her unparalleled agility and precision earned her the moniker "Scout." Now, she brings her deadly swiftness to the Last Flag arena, proving that the most dangerous threats are the ones you never see coming.
